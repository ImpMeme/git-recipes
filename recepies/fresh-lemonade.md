**Ingredients:**

- 4 lemons
- 1 liter cold water
- 100g sugar
- Ice cubes
- Fresh mint leaves (optional)

**Instructions:**

1. Squeeze the juice from the lemons into a pitcher.
2. Add sugar and mix until dissolved.
3. Pour in cold water and stir well.
4. Add ice cubes and garnish with mint leaves.
5. Serve chilled.
